About Me

Hello, I’m Sagar Makar. I come from a strong technical background, having completed my B.Tech from NIIT University, after which I joined IBM as a cybersecurity consultant. I’m driven by a commitment to continuous growth and excellence. I’m looking forward to leveraging my technical expertise, combined with business acumen, to make meaningful contributions in a leadership role, ultimately driving impactful results for my organization.
